lie as a bug

4.5ft x 11ft, Cyanotype on Canvas, 2023

“lie as a bug” explores the relationships between the perceiver, the creation, and the maker, while also addressing the artist's complex struggles with their physical body. Through this piece the artist invites the viewer to intimately observe their struggle, creating a vulnerable and thought-provoking experience that encourages reflection while literally ‘looking down on’ the documented figure of the artist’s body. “lie as a bug” explores themes of gender dysphoria and disability.


LIE AS A BUG

6.5" x 11" (40 Page Book), Cyanotype on Cotton Rag, 2023

“LIE AS A BUG” is a hand bound 40 page book of cyanotype printed self portrait figure drawings, and bug studies.
